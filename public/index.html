Privacy Policy

About Us

We are KodeVana, a freelancing agency located in BTR Assam, India. Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality digital solutions. We have developed the BFP app (Bodoland Peoples' Front) for the political party Bodoland Peoples' Front, aiming to enhance user engagement and communication.

Data Collection

The BFP app allows users to consume content such as daily posts, party events, and achievements without logging in. However, certain features such as donate and join the party require users to log in and provide user details. The collected data are only used for user verification purposes, distinguishing between party members and general users who log in to the app. We ensure that the data collection process is transparent and users are informed about the types of data being collected and the purposes for which it will be used.

Data Usage

All user data collected by the BFP app are strictly used within the app for user verification purposes. This includes determining whether a user is a party member or a general user. We do not share any user data with third parties. Additionally, we take appropriate measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of user data. Our team regularly reviews and updates our security practices to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of user information.
